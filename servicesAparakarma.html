    Aparakarma

    Apara Karmas are Rituals done after the death of an individual, as well as, Rituals done on a Periodical Basis known as Shraddha for the Ancestors in the Pitru Loka. Apara Karmas have to be don with the help of a Priest. During these days the Soul of the dead Person stays in the House. On the 12th day the Soul leaves the House on its Long Journey to Pitru Loka. 13 th day is the conclusion of these long Ceremonies and is a day of Auspicious occasion. The House Purification is done today with Punyahavachana and other related Veda Mantras.
