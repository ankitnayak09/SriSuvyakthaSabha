    Shradha

    The Shradha Ceremony is to pay honour to one's ancestors, particularly the lost parents, rituals and ceremonies are done. The Shraddha rites are performed to ensure that our parents' journey after death is comfortable and stress-free. It's a method of showing appreciation for what they have and praying for the tranquilly of the departed parent.
    The Hindu shraddha ceremony rites are done separately for both mother and father on their respective "thithi," according to the Hindu calendar. Ceremonies are held for the entire community of "Pitr" during the Pitru Paksha or Shradha Paksha.
